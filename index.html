Yamin Li

Computer Science . Vanderbilt University.

Nashville, TN, USA

Hello! This is Yamin (雅敏). I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Neurdy Lab, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Catie Chang. Before joining Vandy, I obtained my master’s degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Xiaoli Guo and Prof. Shanbao Tong, and my bachelor’s degree at Xi’an Jiaotong University, working with Prof. Gang Wang. I am currently a Research Scientist Intern at Meta Reality Lab Research, working on multimodal hand foundation model and egocentric representation learning.

My research interest lies in:

  • Multimodal representation learning
  • AI for health and sensing
  • Vision-language(-action) learning
  • NeuroAI & Brain decoding
  • Large-scale time-series modeling

I’m open to research collaborations and actively seeking 2026 internship opportunities! If you’re interested in working together, feel free to reach out at yamin.li [at] vanderbilt.edu :D !
