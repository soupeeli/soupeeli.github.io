Yamin Li

Computer Science . Vanderbilt University.

Nashville, TN, USA

Hello! This is Yamin (雅敏). I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in Neurdy Lab, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Catie Chang. Before joining Vandy, I obtained my master’s degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Xiaoli Guo and Prof. Shanbao Tong, and my bachelor’s degree at Xi’an Jiaotong University, working with Prof. Gang Wang.

My research interest lies in:

  • NeuroAI
  • Time Series Analysis
  • Multi-modal Learning
  • Brain Decoding
  • Representation Learning
